Frequently Asked Questions about the Life In The UK test

How much does the Life in the UK test cost?

The Life in the UK test costs Â£50. This fee is non-refundable, so make sure you are fully prepared before booking your test.

How long is the Life in the UK test valid?

The Life in the UK test certificate does not expire. Once you pass the test, your certificate remains valid indefinitely.

Where can I take the Life in the UK test?

You can take the Life in the UK test at one of the approved test centres across the UK. When booking your test, you will be able to choose a test centre that is convenient for you. Visit the official Life in the UK Test website to find a test centre near you.

Who needs to take the Life in the UK test?

The Life in the UK test is required for individuals applying for:

  • British citizenship
  • Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)

Some exemptions apply, such as for individuals under 18 or over 65 years of age, or those with certain medical conditions. Check the official guidance for more details.
